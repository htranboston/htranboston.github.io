Huy M. Tran

M.Arch | Experienced Designer | Front-end Developer

Hello, my name is Huy M. Tran. I am an experienced exhibit designer, with an M.Arch degree in Architecture, and 10+ years of professional experience in the exhibit and trade show industry. My specialty is in custom modular exhibits. I am practical and knowledgeable in all phases of design, from concept, to design development and construction documentation. My go to programs are 3D Studio Max, Sketchup, Vectorworks Designer and Adobe Creative Suite.

Currently, besides working on cool exhibitions, I have been teaching myself HTML, CSS and Javascript. My goal is to add web design, interactive experience and front-end developing into my already awesome career.

Specialties: Sketching, Conceptual Design, Structural Design, Graphic Design, 3D Modeling, 3D Rendering, CAD Drafting and Detailing, Creative Thinking, 3D Studio Max, Sketchup, Vectorworks Designer, Adobe Creative Suite, SEO, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Wordpress.
